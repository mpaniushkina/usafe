What is a Connection?
A connection makes it possible for you to receive requests like logins, transactions and other interactions so that you can handle them with 100% security.
A secure connection can be established with any Covr partner. Covr partners include bank websites, e-mail and cloud servers, social media sites, e-commerce sites or other products that have selected Covr as a partner for ultimate security and for your peace of mind.
All of your secure connections are listed in the Connections list found under Menu/Connections.
