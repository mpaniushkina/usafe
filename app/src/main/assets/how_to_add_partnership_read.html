How to add a Connection?
You will initiate the secure connection process from a Covr partnerâ€™s site. After which you will receive a push notification to your mobile device with a verification code.
This is part of the process that will establish a secure connection between you and the partner through the Covr servers. After you receive the push notification you must accept the request to complete the secure connection with our servers. If you did not initiate this request you should decline it.
All your secure connections are listed in the Connections list found under Menu/Connections.
