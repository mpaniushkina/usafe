What is the Quick Bar?
Quick Bar is a part of the Secure Storage (a secure place for storing personal information). Add the most important information to your Quick Bar for easy access.
How does it work?
Each piece of information you add to the Secure Storage has a Star icon next to its title. Just click that to save it to your Quick Bar. It's that easy!
What happens if I want to remove an item from my Quick Bar?
Remove an information piece from the Quick Bar by opening it (Note or Payment Card) and click on the Star icon "Remove from Quick Bar". Done!
What can I put in the Quick Bar?
Anything! All your saved Notes and Payment Card entries, can be added to your Quick Bar via the star icon. Just pick the ones you want to access most often.
