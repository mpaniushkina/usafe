What is Secure Storage?
The Secure Storage function in the Covr app is a place where you can store important personal information, such as passwords, pin codes and other confidential information.
What can I store?
  • Notes e.g pin codes, passwords etc that you find hard to remember
  • Payment Cards - store card information for easier access when shopping or making payments
How secure is my information?
Your information is secure due to the following reasons:
  • Only you have the pin code to access your Covr app.
  • The information you save is only stored locally on your device, behind the Covr app login
  • The information stored on your device is encrypted through a Public Key Infrastructure and only you have access to decrypt your data.
Benefits
Any information entered into your Covr app Secure Storage is securely saved on your device.
You can safely store information such as passwords, username, card information and pin codes because only you can access the Covr app on your phone.
A great additional feature is the "Quick Bar", where information you need and/or use frequently can be placed for easy and quick access with one single step.
